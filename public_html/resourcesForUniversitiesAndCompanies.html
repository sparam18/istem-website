Resources for Universities and Companies

We will not be able to achieve inclusion in STEM fields unless universities and companies want to make this happen. We are excited that several universities and companies are now embracing diversity, and want to change their policies and practices to become more accessible. eleven companies participated at our hackathon in January 2018, and it was great to see the passion of both employees who participated in the hackathon as well as company representatives. We are also seeing great interest from university officials. Together, we are confident that we can make STEM more inclusive and accessible for all!

We are planning programs to involve universities and companies. Get involved with our community for more updates or send us an email at info@inclusivestem.org.
